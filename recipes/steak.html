Brynn's Ribeye Steak

Description

Thick cut ribeye made with garlic, thyme, and butter!

Incredients

  • 16 Ounce Ribeye
  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter
  • Thyme
  • Garlic Cloves, Amount as Desired
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare cuts of steak for seasoning before cooking by salting and peppering. Let sit for 20 minutes.
  2. After seasoning, peel garlic cloves and mash to desired amount.
  3. Cut tablespoons of butter and have ready for cooking.
  4. Prepare sides of your choosing beforehand, so that you may enjoy your steak hot!
  5. Heat pan on stove to highest setting. Add olive oil once heated and slightly smoking.
  6. Sear steak on both sides, cooking to desired temperature.
  7. Half way through cooking, add garlic to pan. Keep it in the oil and rub all over the steak as you cook.
  8. Shortly after adding garlic cloves, add thyme and do the same thing.
  9. As you finish cooking your steak, add butter and baste for a few moments.
  10. Remove steak from pan and insert into mouth! Enjoy!
